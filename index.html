Cards: The Art of Design, the Magic of Playing Cards

Welcome to Cards, the place where a passion for design meets the timeless charm of playing cards. Explore unique decks, dive into their rich history, and get inspired by interactive activities. We're here to create, connect, and share excellence in the world of graphics and playing cards.

About us

Our museum was born from the desire to tell the story of playing cards not merely as gaming objects, but as true works of design. Every deck holds a story: centuries-old traditions, artistic evolution, cultural symbols, and creative visions that cross eras and borders. Through this space, we aim to celebrate the work of artists, graphic designers, and illustrators who have transformed a simple gaming tool into a powerful and recognizable means of expression. From European courts to contemporary independent design decks, playing cards reflect society, aesthetics, and the collective imagination.

The museum seeks to be a meeting point between past and present: a place where history engages in dialogue with innovation, where tradition inspires new creative forms, and where enthusiasts, curious visitors, and designers can discover the magic hidden behind every card. We don't just tell the story of decks. We tell the story of ideas, cultures, symbols, and creativity.

Who we are

We are five university students brought together by a shared passion for design, visual culture, and the timeless world of playing cards. What began in 2024 as an academic project quickly became something more: a creative space where history, art, and graphic experimentation could meet. We created this museum to show that playing cards are not just tools for games, but powerful design objects shaped by centuries of culture, symbolism, and artistic vision. Through this project, we want to explore their evolution, highlight the artists behind them, and inspire others to see cards from a new perspective. This museum is our way of connecting creativity with tradition - and sharing that discovery with the world.
