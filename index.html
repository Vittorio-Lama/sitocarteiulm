About us

Our museum was born from the desire to tell the story of playing cards not just as simple gaming tools, but as true design objects. Each deck carries a narrative made of centuries-old traditions, artistic evolution, and cultural symbols.

About us

The museum aims to be a meeting point between past and present: a space where tradition inspires innovation and where design evolves through time.

Le radici lontane e misteriose

Il viaggio delle carte inizia in Oriente, con le prime attestazioni che risalgono alla Cina del IX secolo. Inizialmente non erano solo strumenti di gioco, ma anche supporti simbolici e culturali.
